Common questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link shortly.

How are points calculated?

Points are awarded based on how accurately you predict match results. The more precise your prediction, the more points you earn.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion and removal of your data, email us at vlad@browsepedia.com with the subject line "Delete my account".
