Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

This policy describes how Browsepedia Application Utilities S.R.L. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Garage Bet (the "App").

1. Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. This information is required to identify your account and allow you to use the App's prediction features.

2. How we use your information

We use your name and email address to:

  • create and manage your account
  • associate your predictions with your profile
  • send you account-related communications (e.g. password reset)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely and protected using industry-standard measures. We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-party services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services in the App.

5. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request deletion of your data
  • withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
